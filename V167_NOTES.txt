V167 NBA EMAIL URL NOTES

Added routes:
- /api/test-nba-brackets-email?to=you@example.com
- /api/send-nba-brackets-email-batch?offset=0&limit=25&pauseMs=1200

Behavior:
- Both routes require you to be signed in as the admin user.
- Test route sends one copy of the NBA playoff email.
- Batch route sends a slow batch one email at a time, with a pause between sends.
- Recipients are built from marketing_leads (opted in and not unsubscribed) plus users emails, deduped.

Suggested usage:
1) Test to yourself:
   /api/test-nba-brackets-email?to=YOUR_EMAIL
2) Send first batch:
   /api/send-nba-brackets-email-batch?offset=0&limit=25&pauseMs=1200
3) Open the Next batch URL shown in the response until Has more = no.
